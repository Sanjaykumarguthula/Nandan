BUSINESS / PARTNERSHIP

At Nandan Therapeutics, we specialize in fostering strong business development and partnership opportunities within the pharmaceutical industry. Our focus is on enabling seamless market access, ensuring reliable sourcing and supply, and facilitating regulatory compliance across multiple regions. Through our extensive network, rigorous due diligence, and deep industry expertise, we empower pharmaceutical manufacturers, distributors, and healthcare providers to navigate complex markets and expand their global footprint.

Our commitment to quality, compliance, and efficiency drives every aspect of our operations. Whether through market access partnerships, international sourcing, reference drug supply, or tender support, we ensure that essential medicines and healthcare solutions reach the people who need them most.

Market Access Partnerships

We highly prioritize in collaborating with pharmaceutical manufacturers, actively supporting their expansion into the UK EU, UK and international markets, by leveraging our extensive network of trusted local partners.

With a strong presence in regions such as the UK, Europe, Africa, Asia & Middle East, we provide expertise and distribution channels to facilitate market entry.

Our local partners undergo thorough internal due diligence and compliance checks, including on-site audits, to ensure alignment with our stringent quality and ethical standards. This guarantees their ability to operate legally and ethically within our highly regulated industry.

International Sourcing and Supplies

Nandan Therapeutics utilizes its global supplier network to efficiently meet your needs, whether for long-term supply or to address market shortages, all at competitive prices.

Our suppliers undergo rigorous internal due diligence, compliance checks, and on-site audits to ensure adherence to our strict quality and compliance standards

Leveraging trusted logistic partners, we distribute both Cold-chain and ambient goods to countries worldwide

Reference Listed Drug Supply

A Reference Listed Drug (RLD) serves as the standard for new generic versions, ensuring bioequivalence.

Companies must reference the RLD in their regulatory submissions for generic approval. Nandan Therapeutics sources and supplies RLDs internationally.

Designating an RLD ensures therapeutic equivalence across various parameters.

Nandan Therapeutics aids in the development of generic drugs by facilitating global market access.

Unlicensed Medicines and Specials Supplies

If a product lacking a marketing authorization (MA), we leverage our global network of suppliers to efficiently source these products to meet unmet medical needs.

We actively cater to Hospitals and Pharmacies seeking to fulfil prescriptions for unlicensed medications prescribed by clinicians.

Our goal is to facilitate pharmacies and clinicians worldwide in providing essential medicines to their patients promptly. Whether it's a single shipment or regular deliveries of multiple containers, we ensure timely access to necessary medications.

Additionally, we offer customized and specially prepared medicines prescribed by healthcare professionals when licensed products are unavailable. For the products without any MA, we tap into our compounder network and specialized manufacturers to source and supply these "specials". Each inquiry is handled with meticulous attention and urgency to swiftly provide these medications to patients in need."

Tender support and supply

Our enduring partnerships with manufacturers and validated suppliers afford us the opportunity to engage in a broad spectrum of pharmaceutical tenders. Leveraging our expertise in sourcing and distribution, we guarantee competitive pricing and reliable supply, meeting stringent tender deadlines. Additionally, we collaborate closely with our local partners to facilitate their participation in restricted tenders exclusively available to native companies. This approach has significantly enhanced our success rate and increased our likelihood of securing supply contracts in international tenders.
