About Us

Discover Nandan Therapeutics’ journey, expertise, and commitment to delivering premium pharmaceutical solutions worldwide.

Who We Are

Nandan Therapeutics is a specialty pharmaceutical and medical device company with deep expertise in global market access. We offer a portfolio of differentiated, value-added products that enhance patient care, with footprint across Europe, India, the Middle East, and Africa. Our products are manufactured in world-class facilities compliant with MHRA, US FDA, WHO, and EU GMP Standards.

We offer a diverse portfolio that includes specialty pharmaceuticals —such as Sustained-release Formulations, Transdermal patches, and Hormonal therapies—as well as innovative medical devices, with a focus on segments like Hemostasis, Aesthetics and other healthcare products.

We serve wholesale distributors, hospitals, retail pharmacies, and government healthcare sectors with professionalism and reliability, striving to make a meaningful impact on global health. Nandan Therapeutics operates under the regulation of the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA), ensuring full compliance with Good Distribution Practice (GDP) standards across all our operations.
